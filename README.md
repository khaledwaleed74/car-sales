#  Power BI Car Sales Dashboard

Interactive Power BI dashboard designed to track key performance metrics and uncover actionable insights for car sales performance analysis.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- YTD Total Sales: $371.2M (+23.59% YoY)
- MTD Total Sales: $54.3M
- YTD Cars Sold: 13.3K (+19.73% YoY)
- Average Price: $28.0K (up to $28.3K MTD)

---

## Dashboard Overview

This dashboard enables detailed analysis of car sales performance across multiple dimensions, including:

- Body style
- Color
- Dealer region
- Time-based trends (weekly, monthly, yearly)

Dynamic filters allow users to drill down into data and explore performance from different perspectives.

---

## Key Insights

- Strong growth in total sales (+23.59% YoY)
- Increase in number of cars sold (+19.73% YoY)
- Stable average price, indicating pricing consistency
- Growth driven primarily by increased sales volume, showing sustainable business performance

---

## Conclusion

The analysis highlights a healthy and growing business with consistent demand and stable pricing strategy, indicating strong and sustainable performance over time.
